ABOUT US

ABOUT US:
The Program has recently Estabalished by our team partners in December 2021. With Our CO-Founder Mr.Ujjwal Tyagi, campaign picked up a great acceleration in last 3 months.Strongly aligned with the ‘Right to have Healthy food Act’. Feed India is committed to the Government’s vision to improve access to Healthy food for poor people. Cooked meals and ration kits are prepared at a subsidized rate of ₹10 per meal. These meals are picked up free of cost by our partner NGOs from kitchen locations and served to beneficiaries. Beneficiaries include children, elderly, women, daily wagers, transgenders and sex workers among others.
VISION:
Feeding India, a non-profit by Ujjwal Tyagi, is dedicated towards making India hunger-free. With the help of our NGOs, kitchens and donors, we are moving one step closer to our goal every day. Together, we believe, we can ensure everyone has access to food daily. MISSION:
We leverage existing community and Government resources to ensure that all are having basic food meals on right time.
GOAL:
Our Primary Goal is to decrease the hnger death rate of india, which is currentlty 36% to 0 by 2023.
